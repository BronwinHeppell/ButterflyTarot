Xerces Blue
EXTINCT
Destroyed by development in the sand dunes of San Francisco. Declared extinct by the 1940s.
The Miami Blue
ENDANGERED
After Hurricane Andrew there were no confirmed reports of the Miami Blue for 7 years. Their population is less than 100 in Florida.
Protocoeliades Kristenseni
EXTINCT
Extinct for 55 Million Years.
The Karner Blue
ENDANGERED
Endangered since 1992. The Karner Blue caterpillars feed only on the leaves of the wild Lapine plant, this severely restricts where they can survive.
The Mitchell's Satyr
ENDANGERED
The Mitchell's Satyr lives in wetlands which receives carbonate-rich ground water from seeps and springs it is very hard to find which critically endangered this butterfly
The Palos Verdes Blue
ENDANGERED
Listed as endangered in 1980, this butterfly's habitat is under constant threat of development and weed control.
